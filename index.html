Social Network Analysis of the Reddit Hyperlink Network

Examining data of 55,863 subreddits that was collected by researchers from Stanford University, my team and I explored network connections using the Exponential Random Graph Model (ERGM).

After applying Newman-Girvan Clustering, we were able to identify community clusters through hyperlink connections. These findings could be applicable for the development of effective advertising with the objective of limiting costs, while simultaneously increasing audience engagement and reach.

Flyer Redesign

Applied key design principles to enhance clarity and visual harmony by resolving alignment issues, introducing subtle yet impactful repetition for cohesion, and optimizing the use of negative space to create a more engaging and functional layout.

Tableu Data Dashboard

Based on the Vlamis Challenge, this dashboard seeks to provide novel insight given a dataset of company sale data. Each visualization represents a key insight(e.g. seasonal patterns or month-to-month sale differnces).

Minerva

Named after the goddess of Wisdom, "Minerva" is an innovative chatbot advisor utilizing Google Gemini, designed to enhance user interaction by leveraging a dynamic knowledge base of user preferences and past interactions. My team and I formulated strategies for data pruning and summarization to optimize storage with ChromaDB and retrieval processes, ensuring quick response times and relevance in user interactions.
